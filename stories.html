What is a curbside vet visit?

Has your local vet or animal clinic cut back services due to COVID-19? Pets still need care during a pandemic. While some health problems are emergencies, you can put more routine care off until later. It all depends on what stage your area is in regarding lockdown or reopening. When do you call your vet for an appointment, when do you wait, and what do you do if you need to take your pet into the clinic? How do you keep your dog or cat healthy and stick to social distancing rules? Here’s what you need to know to keep you and your companion animal safe.
